About me.

I am a Technologist and a Teacher at heart.

I love working while having fun. I also love to learn new things, both personally and technically.

I like participating and coaching at workshops (e.g. Django Girls, Hour of Code, Rails Girls), conferences and communities (e.g. Women Who Code, Women Techmakers, codebar). You can also find me reading, writing, watching movies, taking a walk, having a life talk over coffee with friends or enjoying a wonderful view.
